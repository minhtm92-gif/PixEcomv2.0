1. Gioi thieu he thong

PixEcom v2 la nen tang e-commerce da nguoi ban (multi-tenant), cho phep nhieu seller quan ly cua hang, san pham, don hang va quang cao tren cung mot he thong. He thong bao gom 3 phan chinh:

Seller

Seller Portal

Trang quan ly danh cho nguoi ban. Quan ly don hang, san pham, sellpage, quang cao, analytics.

Admin

Admin Portal

Trang quan tri he thong. Quan ly seller, toan bo don hang, san pham, cai dat nen tang.

Public

Storefront

Trang ban hang cong khai. Khach hang xem san pham, dat hang, thanh toan, theo doi don.

Kien truc ky thuat

  • Frontend: Next.js 14 (React) + Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: NestJS 10 (Node.js) REST API
  • Database: PostgreSQL 16 + Prisma ORM
  • Auth: JWT (15 phut) + Refresh Token (7 ngay, httpOnly cookie)
  • Payment: Stripe + PayPal
  • CDN: Cloudflare R2 tai cdn.pixelxlab.com
  • Email: SendGrid

4. Huong dan Seller Portal

Seller Portal la noi nguoi ban quan ly toan bo hoat dong kinh doanh: don hang, san pham, sellpage (trang ban hang), quang cao Facebook, va analytics. Moi seller co mot cua hang rieng voi URL dang /{store-slug}.

4.1 Dang nhap

  1. Truy cap https://pixecom.pixelxlab.com/login
  2. Nhap Email va Password cua tai khoan seller
  3. Click nut "Sign In"
  4. He thong se chuyen huong den trang Dashboard cua Seller Portal

Luu y: Session se tu dong refresh token sau moi 15 phut. Neu khong thao tac trong 7 ngay, ban se phai dang nhap lai.

4.2 Orders (Don hang)

Trang quan ly don hang cho phep ban xem, loc, tim kiem va xuat du lieu don hang.

Xem

Danh sach don hang hien thi: ma don, ngay tao, khach hang, tong tien, trang thai, nguon (Facebook/Direct).

Loc

Loc theo trang thai (PENDING, CONFIRMED, SHIPPED, DELIVERED, CANCELLED, REFUNDED), nguon don hang, va khoang thoi gian.

Tim kiem

Tim theo ma don hang, ten hoac email khach hang.

Export

Xuat danh sach don hang ra file CSV de xu ly ben ngoai.

Import

Import tracking number hang loat tu file CSV (dung de cap nhat ma van don).

Luong don hang: PENDING → CONFIRMED (thanh toan thanh cong) → SHIPPED (da gui hang) → DELIVERED (da giao). Khi don chuyen sang SHIPPED, email thong bao se tu dong gui cho khach hang.

4.3 Ads Manager

Ket noi tai khoan Facebook de dong bo va xem hieu suat quang cao.

Ket noi FB

Click "Connect Facebook" de xac thuc qua Meta OAuth. Sau khi ket noi, he thong se dong bo du lieu quang cao tu Facebook Ads API.

Chi so

Xem cac chi so: Spend, Revenue, ROAS, CR (Conversion Rate), CR1 (Checkout Rate), CR2 (Purchase Rate), Clicks, Impressions, CPM, CPC.

Cong thuc

CR = purchase / contentView * 100
CR1 = checkout / contentView * 100
CR2 = purchase / checkout * 100
ROAS = revenue / spend

4.4 Campaigns

Xem danh sach cac Facebook Ad Campaigns da dong bo tu Meta. Hien thi trang thai (Active, Paused, Archived), budget, va performance metrics cua tung campaign.

4.5 Analytics

Bao cao tong hop hieu suat kinh doanh cua cua hang.

  • Revenue: Tong doanh thu theo thoi gian
  • Orders: So don hang theo ngay/tuan/thang
  • Conversion: Ti le chuyen doi (CR, CR1, CR2)
  • Top Products: San pham ban chay nhat
  • Traffic Sources: Nguon luu luong (Facebook, Direct, ...)

Quan trong: He thong KHONG tinh trung binh cac ti le. Thay vao do, SUM tong raw counts truoc, roi moi tinh ti le. Vi du: CR = SUM(purchase) / SUM(contentView) * 100.

4.6 Sellpages (Trang ban hang)

Sellpage la trang landing page cho tung san pham, noi khach hang xem chi tiet va mua hang.

Tao moi

Tao sellpage moi voi tieu de, mo ta, hinh anh, gia, va lien ket san pham.

Boost

Cac module tang cuong chuyen doi: countdown timer, stock counter, trust badges, social proof.

URL

Sellpage co URL dang: /{store-slug}/{product-slug}

SEO

He thong tu dong tao meta title, description, va JSON-LD structured data (Product schema) cho SEO.

4.7 Creatives

Quan ly ad creatives va media files. Upload hinh anh/video len CDN (Cloudflare R2) tai cdn.pixelxlab.com. To chuc va gan tag cho cac creative de su dung trong quang cao.

4.8 Products (San pham)

Quan ly danh sach san pham cua cua hang.

  • Thong tin co ban: Ten, mo ta, hinh anh, gia ban, gia goc
  • Variants: Cac bien the san pham (mau sac, kich co, ...)
  • Stock: Quan ly ton kho. Khi khach mua, stock tu dong giam. Khi hoan tien (refund), stock tu dong phuc hoi.
  • Trang thai: Published (hien thi tren storefront) hoac Draft (an)
  • Reviews: Xem va duyet danh gia cua khach hang (PENDING → APPROVED/REJECTED)
  • Stats: Xem so luong don hang va doanh thu cua tung san pham

4.9 Settings (Cai dat)

  • Store Info: Ten cua hang, logo, mo ta, slug
  • Shipping: Cai dat phi van chuyen, vung giao hang
  • Policies: Chinh sach doi tra, bao mat, dieu khoan

5. Huong dan Admin Portal

Admin Portal la trang quan tri danh cho doi ngu van hanh nen tang. Tai day, admin co the quan ly sellers, xem toan bo don hang, san pham, va cau hinh he thong.

5.1 Dang nhap Admin

  1. Truy cap https://pixecom.pixelxlab.com/admin
  2. Nhap Email va Password cua tai khoan admin
  3. Click "Sign In"
  4. He thong su dung endpoint /api/auth/admin-login (khac voi seller login)

Phan quyen: SUPERADMIN co toan quyen. ADMIN co the quan ly sellers va orders. SUPPORT chi xem don hang. CONTENT quan ly noi dung. ANALYST chi xem analytics.

5.3 Quan ly Sellers

  • Xem danh sach: Tat ca seller dang ky tren he thong
  • Duyet seller: Phe duyet seller moi (PENDING → ACTIVE)
  • Tam ngung: Suspend seller vi pham (ACTIVE → SUSPENDED)
  • Chi tiet: Xem thong tin, don hang, san pham, doanh thu cua tung seller

5.4 Orders (Toan he thong)

Xem toan bo don hang tren nen tang, bao gom don cua tat ca sellers. Loc theo seller, trang thai, thoi gian. Admin co the can thiep vao don hang khi can (vi du: xu ly khieu nai, hoan tien).

5.5 Products (Toan he thong)

Xem toan bo san pham tren nen tang. Loc theo seller, trang thai (Published/Draft), danh muc. Admin co the an (unpublish) san pham vi pham chinh sach.

5.6 Platform Settings

  • Payment Gateways: Cau hinh Stripe va PayPal (keys, webhook secrets)
  • Discounts: Tao va quan ly ma giam gia toan nen tang
  • Platform Settings: Cai dat chung: ten nen tang, phi hoa hong, gioi han
  • Staff Management: Quan ly tai khoan staff (ADMIN, SUPPORT, CONTENT, ANALYST)

6. Storefront (Trang ban hang cong khai)

Storefront la trang web cong khai ma khach hang truy cap de xem san pham va mua hang. Khong can dang nhap.

Trang chu cua hang (Store Homepage)

URL: https://pixecom.pixelxlab.com/{store-slug}

Hien thi tat ca san pham da published cua cua hang. Khach hang co the click vao san pham de xem chi tiet.

Vi du cac store:

/techgear-pro /fashionhub /homedecor-plus /gadgetworld

Trang san pham (Sellpage)

URL: https://pixecom.pixelxlab.com/{store-slug}/{product-slug}

Hien thi chi tiet san pham: hinh anh, mo ta, gia, variants, reviews, nut "Buy Now".

Vi du: /techgear-pro/laptop-stand-pro

Danh gia san pham (Reviews)

Khach hang co the xem danh gia cua nguoi mua khac va gui danh gia moi. Danh gia can duoc seller duyet (PENDING → APPROVED) truoc khi hien thi cong khai. Diem trung binh (rating) tu dong cap nhat.

Theo doi don hang (Order Tracking)

Nhap ma don hang va email de xem trang thai don hang, ma van don, va lich su cap nhat.

7. Thanh toan (Payment)

He thong ho tro 2 phuong thuc thanh toan: Stripe (the tin dung/ghi no) va PayPal.

Stripe

Thanh toan bang the (Visa, Mastercard, ...) qua Stripe Elements (CardElement).

The test de thu nghiem:

So the: 4242 4242 4242 4242
Han su dung: 12/28 (bat ky ngay tuong lai)
CVC: 123 (bat ky 3 so)

PayPal

Thanh toan qua tai khoan PayPal. He thong su dung PayPal REST API (server-side).

Che do Sandbox:

Dang chay o che do sandbox (test). Khong xu ly tien that. Nut PayPal se hien thi khi chon phuong thuc thanh toan PayPal tai trang checkout.

Luong thanh toan

1. Chon san pham 2. Buy Now 3. Checkout 4. Thanh toan 5. Xac nhan

Webhooks: He thong su dung Stripe Webhook va PayPal Webhook de xac nhan thanh toan phia server. Tranh tinh trang race condition giua client confirm va webhook bang cach su dung atomic updateMany WHERE status = 'PENDING'.

8. Theme Toggle (Chuyen doi giao dien)

He thong ho tro 2 che do giao dien: Light Mode (sang) va Dark Mode (toi).

Chuyen doi

Click vao icon mat troi/mat trang trong phan header cua sidebar de chuyen doi giua Light va Dark mode.

Luu tru

Tuy chon duoc luu trong localStorage cua trinh duyet. Lan sau truy cap se tu dong ap dung che do da chon.

9. API Endpoints Reference

Base URL: https://api.pixelxlab.com/api

Health Check

GET /api/health → {"status":"ok"}

Authentication

POST /api/auth/login Seller login (email + password)
POST /api/auth/admin-login Admin login (email + password)
POST /api/auth/refresh Refresh JWT token (httpOnly cookie)
POST /api/auth/logout Logout (clear refresh token)

Orders Auth Required

GET /api/orders Danh sach don hang cua seller
GET /api/orders/:id Chi tiet don hang
PATCH /api/orders/:id/status Cap nhat trang thai don hang

Products Auth Required

GET /api/products Danh sach san pham cua seller
POST /api/products Tao san pham moi
PATCH /api/products/:id Cap nhat san pham

Storefront Public

GET /api/storefront/:sellerSlug Store homepage data
GET /api/storefront/:sellerSlug/:sellpageSlug Sellpage detail data
POST /api/storefront/checkout Tao don hang + thanh toan
POST /api/storefront/:slug/reviews Gui danh gia san pham
GET /api/storefront/sitemap-data Data cho sitemap.xml

Admin Admin Auth

GET /api/admin/dashboard Dashboard metrics
GET /api/admin/sellers Danh sach sellers
GET /api/admin/orders Tat ca don hang
GET /api/admin/products Tat ca san pham
